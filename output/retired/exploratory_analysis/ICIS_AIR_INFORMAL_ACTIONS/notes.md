# ICIS-AIR Informal Actions — Exploratory Notes

Explored the informal enforcement actions table. Event-level panel: multiple dated actions per facility over time.

## What we checked

- **Missingness** — coverage by column
- **Agency distribution** — state vs. local vs. EPA

## Key numbers

- **336,410 records** total.
- **Agency:** State-led (266,008, 79%), local (53,285, 16%), EPA (17,117, 5%).
- **Missingness:** Near-complete. `ACHIEVED_DATE` is 0.1% missing. All other fields complete.

## Key takeaways

- Informal actions outnumber formal actions ~3:1 (336k vs. 106k), consistent with the enforcement pyramid — most enforcement starts with warnings and NOVs before escalating.
- State agencies handle an even larger share of informal actions (79%) than formal ones (65%).
